Computer Science and Engineering

DBMS Virtual Laboratory

  1. To understand the fundamental concepts and architecture of Database Management Systems.
  2. To study and compare DBMS with traditional file systems through interactive simulations.
  3. To design Entity Relationship diagrams and map them to relational schemas.
  4. To understand and apply relational algebra operations on database relations.
  5. To write and execute SQL queries including joins, subqueries, and aggregate functions.
  6. To analyze functional dependencies and perform database normalization up to BCNF.
  7. To understand transaction processing, ACID properties, and concurrency control mechanisms.
  8. To visualize database concepts through step-by-step interactive simulations.
  9. To evaluate understanding through pretest, posttest, and quiz questions.
  10. To develop practical database design and query optimization skills.
