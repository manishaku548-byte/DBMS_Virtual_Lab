Experiment 4

ER to Relational Mapping Converter

Aim

To convert ER Model into Relational Tables.

Objective

  • Understand ER Mapping.
  • Convert Entities into Relations.
  • Identify Primary Keys.
  • Create Relational Schema.

Theory

ER diagrams are transformed into relational schemas. Each entity becomes a table. Attributes become columns. Primary Keys uniquely identify records. Relationships are represented using foreign keys.
