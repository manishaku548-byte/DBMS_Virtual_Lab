Experiment 10

Transaction Processing & Concurrency Control Simulator

Aim

To understand transaction processing and concurrency control in DBMS.

Theory

A transaction is a logical unit of work. Transactions follow ACID properties:

  • Atomicity
  • Consistency
  • Isolation
  • Durability

Concurrency Control ensures multiple transactions execute safely without data inconsistency.
