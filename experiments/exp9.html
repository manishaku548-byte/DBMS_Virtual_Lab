Experiment 9

Normalization Simulator

Aim

To understand normalization and convert relations into higher normal forms.

Theory

Normalization is a process of organizing data to reduce redundancy and improve data integrity. Normal Forms:

  • 1NF (First Normal Form)
  • 2NF (Second Normal Form)
  • 3NF (Third Normal Form)
  • BCNF (Boyce Codd Normal Form)
