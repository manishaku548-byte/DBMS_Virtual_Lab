Virtual Database Management System Laboratory

  1. To understand the fundamental concepts and architecture of database management systems.
  2. To visualize the Three Schema Architecture and the role of data independence.
  3. To design Entity Relationship diagrams and map them into relational schemas.
  4. To perform relational algebra operations and analyze their results.
  5. To write and execute SQL queries on sample relations.
  6. To visualize SQL Join operations between related tables.
  7. To analyze functional dependencies and identify candidate keys.
  8. To study normalization and convert relations into higher normal forms.
  9. To understand transaction processing, concurrency control and deadlocks.
  10. To evaluate understanding through pretest, posttest and viva questions.
