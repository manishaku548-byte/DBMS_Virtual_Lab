DBMS Virtual Laboratory

Learn, Simulate, and Master Database Management System Concepts Through Interactive Virtual Experiments

The lab contents are chosen such that they cover the syllabus of Database Management System courses, including fundamental concepts such as DBMS architecture, Entity Relationship modeling, relational algebra, SQL programming, functional dependencies, normalization, transaction processing, and concurrency control. The experiments are designed to provide hands-on experience and strengthen students' understanding of database concepts through practical implementation and interactive simulations.
